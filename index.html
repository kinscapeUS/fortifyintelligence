Regulatory Convergence
The window is closing.

Multiple regulatory deadlines converge in 2026, creating genuine urgency for PE firms with AI exposure across their portfolios.

Jun
2026

Colorado AI Act

Requires risk management programs, impact assessments, and algorithmic discrimination prevention for high-risk AI in employment, housing, credit, and healthcare.

Aug
2026

EU AI Act — Full Application

High-risk AI systems face penalties up to €35M or 7% of global turnover. Extraterritorial reach means any portfolio company with EU exposure must comply.

SEC Examination Priorities

AI is explicitly a top examination priority. Scrutiny of AI-related disclosures, supervisory frameworks, and whether controls match actual practices.

230 control objectives across governance, data, model development, validation, and monitoring. Becoming the de facto standard for financial services.
